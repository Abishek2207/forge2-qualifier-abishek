\# Status Reporter Skill



\## Trigger

When asked to prepare a qualifier report, sprint status, agent update, or progress report.



\## Steps

1\. Summarize completed work.

2\. List remaining work.

3\. Ask for human decision if needed.

4\. Format exactly:

&#x20;  - What I Did

&#x20;  - What's Left

&#x20;  - What Needs Your Call
